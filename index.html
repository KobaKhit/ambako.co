About

Ambako Vachadze is an award-winning greco-roman wrestler in the 66 kg category with many years of experience in high level international competitions. Originally from Georgia he represented Russia internationally.

Ambako has worked with several national wrestling teams as a trainer. Currently, Ambako is accepting offers for coaching positions.
